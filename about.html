Meet the worldwide bsc-blockchain Board. Any account can draw a picture on the board and assign it a link to any site. How it works:

1 - go to paint mode (press Paint-button)

2 - select the section of the Board you want to edit (click on the desired section - a cursor you can move will appear)

3 - make a double click on the cursor - edit-menu will open

4 - in the edit-menu, select a color, enter a link, select pixels

5 - to save - press Save-button and sign the transaction - after a while the changes will take effect
